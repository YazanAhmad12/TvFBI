Academics

The academics at Maya Academy represent a philosophy of hands on learning they teach by showing they guide by doing and they make sure that every student has the chance to practice and to create with her own hands they do not rely only on theory they focus on real application and on making every lesson practical and useful their experience allows them to understand the different levels of students and to adjust their approach so that every woman whether beginner or advanced feels included and supported

Academic Pathways

Hand Sewing Pathway

This pathway focuses on mastering basic and advanced hand sewing techniques students learn stitches embroidery and repair skills it is ideal for beginners who want to build a strong foundation and create practical projects with their hands

Home Sewing Pathway

This pathway is designed for those who want to become confident using a sewing machine students learn how to make garments household items and simple fashion projects it prepares them to work from home or start small creative businesses

Fashion Design Pathway

This pathway emphasizes creativity and design skills students learn how to draw models choose fabrics coordinate colors and turn sketches into real clothing it is perfect for those who want to pursue professional fashion design or develop their own brand
