Admissions

Maya Academy is open to all women and girls who are motivated to learn sewing and fashion design the process is simple and flexible students can apply directly by contacting the academy choosing the course that matches their goals and registering for the upcoming session our admission philosophy is to make education accessible and supportive so that every student feels welcome regardless of her background or level of experience the goal is to give every woman the chance to join a community of learning creativity and empowerment

Begin Your Academic Journey
