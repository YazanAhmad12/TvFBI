Success Stories

Sara

Sara joined Maya Academy in 2019 with no background in sewing after completing the home sewing course she started creating clothes for her family and friends today she runs a small online store where she sells handmade dresses and household items and she is proud to be financially independent

Omar

Omar joined the academy in 2020 with an interest in learning practical skills he mastered machine sewing and tailoring during his course after graduation he started working in a local tailoring shop where he applies his skills every day and supports his family with a stable income

Amani

Amani enrolled in the fashion design pathway in 2021 her passion for creativity grew with every project she completed after graduating she was invited to present her designs in a local university event and now she dreams of building her own fashion brand that reflects her style and cultural identity
