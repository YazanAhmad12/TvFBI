Annual Exhibition

Maya Academy hosts an annual exhibition to showcase the creativity and skills of our students the exhibition features handmade garments design projects and innovative creations from all programs it provides an opportunity for students to present their work to the community parents and potential partners the event also includes live demonstrations networking opportunities and awards to recognize outstanding talent and encourage continued growth and excellence among our students

Event Highlights

  • Showcasing student projects and creative designs
  • Live sewing and fashion demonstrations by trainers
  • Award ceremonies recognizing outstanding achievements
  • Networking opportunities with alumni and community members
  • Interactive workshops for attendees to try skills and techniques
